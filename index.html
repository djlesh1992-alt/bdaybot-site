How it works
Three steps.
That's genuinely it.
1

Start a chat with Bday Bot

Tap the button above, say hi, and save Bday Bot as a contact. Everything happens in WhatsApp — no app, no download, no passwords.

2

Forward your friends' contact cards

Tap the + button in WhatsApp, select as many contacts as you like, and send. Bday Bot reaches out to each of them and asks for their birthday — so you don't have to.

3

Wake up to a reminder on the day

Every morning someone's birthday arrives, you get a WhatsApp message at 7am local time with a one-tap link to message them directly. Job done.
